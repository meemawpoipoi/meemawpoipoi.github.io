For those trying to get out there...


My name is Adeline Trautman and I am a Visual Arts graduate from Bob Jones University. I have always wanted to connect with people using my artwork and one of the best ways to do that is by hosting an event open to the public where people who appreciate beauty and unique creativity can come and connect with artists and their work. I created a 10-step guide based on my journey through organizing my own art show top help other art students do the same thing on their own.




You are not alone!

As a young artist just coming out of college, it can feel like everyone else has the information and resources before you had a chance to learn. The truth is, there are hundreds of new artists struggling to find where to start. Members in the art community love to support each other, and genuine artists freely share what experience and resources they have if you know who to ask.


Get out there!


The best way to begin processes like this is to just jump in and start reaching out! Start with the resources you have and don't be afraid to see where you go from there. Comfort zones will be broken, but that's the best way to make exciting things happen!
